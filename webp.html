EthioTour is your gateway to exploring the rich heritage and stunning landscapes of Ethiopia. Come and experience the warmth of Ethiopian hospitality and the wonders of this incredible country with EthioTour. Meskel/ September 27

Registered with UNESCO, Meskel is a major Ethiopian Orthodox festival commemorating the discovery of the True Cross through the lighting of massive Damera bonfires, traditional chanting, and grand celebrations nationwide.
